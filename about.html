Company Overview

Clean Energy. Smart Investment. Long-Term Impact.

SunGarner Green Asset Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in June 2024 as a wholly owned subsidiary of SunGarner Energies Ltd., focused on establishing and managing independent renewable power assets across India. As an Independent Power Producer (IPP) we develop and operate large-scale solar and hybrid projects under CAPEX, OPEX and RESCO models to enable clients to shift to green energy without upfront capital costs.

Vision

To be a leading force in India’s renewable energy transformation by developing sustainable, innovative and impactful energy assets.

Mission

To design, develop and deliver reliable green energy solutions—empowering industries and institutions to meet energy needs sustainably while reducing carbon footprints and operational costs.

Core Values
  • Sustainability First: Long-term eco-friendly solutions that reduce emissions.
  • Engineering Excellence: Robust engineering and precision execution.
  • Customer Commitment: Tailored, cost-effective models with responsive support.
  • Integrity & Accountability: Honest, transparent operations and delivery.
  • Impact-Driven Growth: Measuring success by environmental and social outcomes.

Our Services
IPP (Independent Power Producer)

IPP is a zero-investment solution where SG Green Asset develops and operates grid-connected solar projects and supplies clean energy to businesses via long-term PPAs—no upfront capital required.

Learn about IPP

CPP (Captive Power Plant)

We design, develop, transfer and maintain grid-connected captive solar projects. Captive plants reduce electricity costs and can be paired with O&M services for long-term performance and annuity revenue.
